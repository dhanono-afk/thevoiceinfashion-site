About

The Voice in Fashion is an electronic dance and freestyle act from Miami, Florida, active since 1985. Their debut single “All We Need” (1985) reached #1 on WVUM college radio. The group then broke through nationally with “Only In The Night” (1986), produced by Lewis Martinee (of Exposé fame), which reached #2 on Billboard’s Dance charts and led to extensive national touring. Their international hit “Give Me Your Love” (1988) brought global recognition. After a hiatus, The Voice in Fashion returned in 2012 with The Moment of Truth and have released continuously since, including Bleeding Heart (2019), Before the Sun (2020), Stages (2022), You Make It Better (2023), Chained (2024), and the latest EP “Killer” (2026). Over their career the group has collaborated with Pet Shop Boys, shared the stage with When In Rome, and been produced by Lewis Martinee — the legendary Miami producer behind Exposé.

FAQ

What genre is The Voice in Fashion?+

The Voice in Fashion makes electronic dance music spanning freestyle, synthpop, and club/EDM. Active since 1985, they charted #2 on Billboard's Dance charts with "Only In The Night" (1986).

Where is The Voice in Fashion from?+

The Voice in Fashion is based in Miami, Florida, United States.

What is their latest release?+

The latest release is the EP “Killer” (2026). More releases are on the way.

Has The Voice in Fashion toured?+

Yes. The Voice in Fashion toured extensively across the United States from 1985 to 1991, toured Brazil in 1991, and returned to Brazil in 2023 alongside Miami freestyle legend Stevie B.

Who has The Voice in Fashion collaborated with?+

The Voice in Fashion has collaborated with Pet Shop Boys, shared the stage with When In Rome, and their breakthrough hit "Only In The Night" (1986) was produced by Lewis Martinee — the Miami producer behind Exposé. Their 2025 EP "Domino Dancing" revisits the Pet Shop Boys connection with a new interpretation of the classic track.
